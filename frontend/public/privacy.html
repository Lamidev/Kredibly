Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Kredibly. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our platform and our WhatsApp assistant, Kreddy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us through the Kredibly dashboard and through our WhatsApp assistant. This includes:

  • Business details (Name, Display Name, Entity Type)
  • Contact information (Email, WhatsApp Phone Number)
  • Transaction data (Sales records, Customer names, Payment status)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and maintain our services, allow Kreddy to process your commands and record sales, send payment reminders, and improve our platform's AI.

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your data is stored on secure servers and is never shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. WhatsApp Integration

By linking your WhatsApp number to Kredibly, you consent to our WhatsApp assistant processing your messages to manage your sales records.
